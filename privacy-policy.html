隐私政策

DCheck - 每日事项

最后更新：2025年2月

核心承诺：DCheck 尊重并保护您的隐私。我们不收集、不存储、不传输您的任何个人数据。

数据存储

DCheck 的所有数据均存储在您的设备本地：

  • 任务数据存储在设备本地的 SQLite 数据库中
  • 设置信息（语言偏好、通知设置）存储在本地
  • 所有数据仅保留在您的设备上，不会上传到任何服务器

网络访问

DCheck 不需要网络连接即可正常运行。应用不会：

  • 连接任何远程服务器
  • 发送任何数据到第三方
  • 进行任何形式的数据追踪

通知权限

如果您启用任务提醒功能，应用会请求通知权限。此权限仅用于：

  • 在任务超时时发送本地提醒通知
  • 通知内容完全基于您本地的任务数据生成

数据删除

卸载应用将自动删除所有本地存储的数据。您也可以在应用内删除特定任务。

儿童隐私

DCheck 不收集任何用户的个人信息，适合所有年龄段用户使用。

政策更新

Privacy Policy

DCheck - Daily Tasks

Last Updated: February 2025

Our Commitment: DCheck respects and protects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Data Storage

All DCheck data is stored locally on your device:

  • Task data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device
  • Settings (language preference, notification settings) are stored locally
  • All data remains on your device and is never uploaded to any server

Network Access

DCheck does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not:

  • Connect to any remote servers
  • Send any data to third parties
  • Perform any form of data tracking

Notification Permission

If you enable task reminders, the app will request notification permission. This permission is only used to:

  • Send local reminder notifications when tasks are overdue
  • Notification content is generated entirely from your local task data

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app will automatically delete all locally stored data. You can also delete specific tasks within the app.

Children's Privacy

DCheck does not collect any personal information from users and is suitable for users of all ages.

Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page. We recommend checking periodically.
