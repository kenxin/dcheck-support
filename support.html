常见问题

如何添加新任务？
进入编辑页面，点击右上角的绿色"+"按钮即可添加新任务。每天最多可添加6个任务。
任务每天会自动重置吗？
是的，每天凌晨2点，所有已完成的任务会自动重置为未完成状态，让您开始新的一天。
如何设置任务提醒？
在设置页面启用通知功能，然后为任务设置结束时间。任务超时后会自动发送提醒通知。
如何调整任务顺序？
在编辑页面，长按任务项并拖拽即可调整顺序。
我的数据安全吗？
完全安全。所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。详情请查看我们的隐私政策。

FAQ

How do I add a new task?
Go to the edit page and tap the green "+" button in the top right corner. You can add up to 6 tasks per day.
Do tasks reset automatically every day?
Yes, at 2 AM every day, all completed tasks are automatically reset to incomplete status for a fresh start.
How do I set up task reminders?
Enable notifications in Settings, then set an end time for your task. You'll receive reminder notifications when tasks are overdue.
How do I reorder tasks?
On the edit page, long press on a task and drag it to adjust the order.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All data is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
